Model Description: This model represents an abstraction of detailed design requirements for an Advanced Autopilot System interacting with an independent sensor platform. The Finite State Machine (FSM) represents a cyber-physical system with two independent components executing in real time for the purpose of ensuring a safe automatic operation in the vicinity of hazardous obstacles. The autopilot system, tightly integrated with the vehicle flight control computer, is responsible for commanding a safety maneuver in the event of a hazard. The sensor is the reporting agent to the autopilot with observability on imminent danger. When the conditions are met to allow an autopilot operation (supported and no failures) and the pilot is not manually controlling the vehicle (standby is false), the autopilot will behave normally until a maneuver is required in the event of a hazard (sensor is not good).
